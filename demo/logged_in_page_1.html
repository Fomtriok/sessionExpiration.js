The two logged in pages in this Demo, are to show that sessionExpiration acts in all tabs at once. Meaning; you don't get logged out in one tab, if you are active in any other tab with the following lines of code.

Change parameters "idleMinutes" and "warningMinutes" yourself. Right now, they are set to logout after 0.2 idle minutes (12 seconds), but already after 0.05 minutes (3 seconds) the red warning banner starts displaying. As soon as you interact with mouse or keyboard, the banner disappears, and the countdown restarts from 0.

For the demo to work, you have to set the correct paths to sessionExpiration.js, as well as to logged_out.html.
